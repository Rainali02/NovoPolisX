Tourist Places in Nagpur

Discover amazing tourist attractions that you can't miss!

Deekshabhoomi

A significant pilgrimage site for Buddhists, Deekshabhoomi is where Dr. B.R. Ambedkar converted to Buddhism.

Raman Science Centre

This interactive science museum promotes scientific knowledge through engaging exhibits and demonstrations.

Futala Lake

A picturesque lake known for its stunning views, especially at sunset. It’s a great spot for leisurely strolls.

Ambazari Lake

This beautiful lake is ideal for recreational activities and features a park perfect for families.

Zero Mile Stone

This historical monument marks the geographical center of India. It’s a beautiful spot to learn about the history of Nagpur.

Mahatma Gandhi Park

A lovely park ideal for relaxation and leisure activities, featuring beautiful greenery and walking paths.

City Center Mall

One of the largest shopping destinations in Nagpur, featuring various brands, restaurants, and entertainment options.

Empress Mall

A popular mall featuring numerous shops, food courts, and a cinema. A great place for entertainment and shopping.

Jain Mandir

A beautiful Jain temple known for its exquisite architecture and serene environment.

Ramtek Fort

A historic fort offering panoramic views and a glimpse into the rich history of the region.

Maharaj Bagh Zoo

A historical garden and zoo showcasing a variety of animals and birds, perfect for family outings.
